About Us

Maweheb is an app that connects talented soccer players with sports clubs and talent scouts.

Players can find out the latest info about available trials and opportunities as well as upload videos of them playing football for talent scouts to view.

Clubs can use the app to review potential players and promote trials and opportunities available to eager Egyptian footballers.
